About Me

Growing up in the 2000s and 2010s, I essentially grew up alongside technology as we know it today. Some of my earliest memories are watching cable TV on a CRT television, and playing around on a computer with a CRT monitor that probably had a hard drive too small to contain a single HD movie file. As such I’ve always had an affinity for technology and, by extension, the arts that new technology enables.

In high school I took an interest in classes where I could work with them, such as Computer Graphics and Computer Science. Today I’m continuing to improve my skills in various fields of graphic design, with a focus on motion graphics and branding. Doing a bit of animation and video editing as a hobby in my free time gave me a foundation to build on in motion graphics, and my attention to detail is put to use in logos, branding and typography.
